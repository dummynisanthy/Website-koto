What is ICT?

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) refers to digital technologies used for communication, data processing, and automation. It includes computers, the internet, and software applications.

Importance of ICT in Education

ICT improves learning by providing access to e-books, online courses, and interactive platforms. It enhances digital literacy and fosters collaborative learning.

Applications of ICT

ICT is widely used in education, business, healthcare, and entertainment. Common applications include cloud computing, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence.

Extra Information

ICT plays a vital role in the Philippines, driving digital transformation in businesses and education.
