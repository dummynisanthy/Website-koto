    ICT in Grade 10

    The Grade 10 Information and Communication Technology (ICT) curriculum aims to equip students with a foundational understanding of how technology works and how it can be used effectively. The course covers a broad range of topics that prepare students for a digital world, emphasizing both technical skills and ethical awareness in using technology

    Key Topics in Grade 10 ICT

    Throughout the year, students will explore a variety of subjects that range from the fundamentals of web development to the basics of networking and online security. The following topics are crucial in building their skills:

    • Introduction to HTML: Students will learn the basics of web development by writing HTML to create website structures and to style them.     • Cybersecurity Awareness: In today's digital age, protecting personal data is critical. Students will learn about common online threats such as viruses, phishing, and hacking. The course also covers best practices for safeguarding information and recognizing security vulnerabilities
